Types of Hackers!


“Hackers often describe what they do as playfully creative problem solving.”
- Heather Brooke


Hacking is compromising digital devices by misusing them by going into networks using unauthorized access to a system. It is illegal and is done by cybercriminals however there are good types of hackers.

Illegal reasons why someone may participate in hacking are:
  1. 1. Revenge
  2. 2. Boredom
  3. 3. Theft
  4. 4. Financial Gain
  5. 5. Espionage
  6. 6. Sabotage
  7. 7. Vandalism
  8. 8. Blackmail


Hackers steal data about your identity, your banking info and they use it for different things such as taking money, taking identities, making false claims and even transferring money. Through the media it is usually portrayed that there is the typical hacker that plots evil plans but in reality there are so many different types of hackers.

Types of Hackers:
  1. 1. White Hat
  2. 2. Black Hat
  3. 3. Grey Hat
  4. 4. Script Kiddies
  5. 5. Green Hat


White hat hackers work for the government, they try to hack into the government systems and databases to find any weak spots in their security. White hat hackers are also known as ethical hackers.

Black hat hackers are hackers who don’t work for the government,they hack into their databases and systems. They steal and take the information they shouldn’t have access to. These types of hackers are criminals.

Grey hat hackers are hackers who fall between black and white hat hackers, they are not certified hackers and work with either good or bad intentions. They don’t want to rob or steal information, they like hacking into loopholes because it is fun for them.

Script kiddies hackers who are amateur hackers. They do not have a lot of knowledge about hacking. They hack into systems, networks and websites using given code. Common reason for their hacking is to get attention from their peers.

Green hat hackers who are currently still learning to hack and they want to become better hackers. They try to learn from experienced hackers.
